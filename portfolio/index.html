TaeKwonDo & American Kickboxing Academy

Courtesy, Integrity, Perserverance, Self-Control, and Indomitable Spirit

We have been providing exceptional training since 2002. Since then, our head coach Tommy Bach has received the US TAEKWONDO CHUNGDOKWAN APPRECIATION AWARD, the 2005 Illinois State Taekwondo Association Scholarship Award, the 2014 Illinois Taekwondo State Championship Certificate of Appreciation, the Illinois TKD Award of Appreciation, and the No Mercy II Award for Outstanding Support and Contribution to Martial Arts.



In 2014 we took our third competition taekwondo team to the Junior Oympics and had three medalists! We are also very proud of our competition boxing team with 7 Chicago Golden Gloves winners. We are proud to say that our main goal and past success has been producing CHAMPIONS!
